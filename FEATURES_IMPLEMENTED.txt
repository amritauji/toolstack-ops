HOW TO USE
==========

1. My Tasks Filter:
   - Click "My Tasks" button in toolbar
   - Shows only your assigned tasks
   - Badge shows count

2. Quick Create:
   - Click floating + button (bottom-right)
   - Or press Ctrl+K
   - Fill title and description
   - Submit

3. Command Palette:
   - Press Ctrl+K or Cmd+K
   - Type to search commands
   - Use arrow keys to navigate
   - Press Enter to execute

4. Task Preview:
   - Hover over any task card
   - See details without clicking
   - Shows priority, assignee, due date

5. Onboarding Tour:
   - Automatically shows on first visit
   - Follow the steps
   - Skip anytime
   - Won't show again after completion
