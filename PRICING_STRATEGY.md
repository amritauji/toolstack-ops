**Target**: Individual users, small teams testing

- Basic features only

### 2. **Starter Plan** - $12/user/month (billed monthly) or $10/user/month (billed annually)
**Target**: Small teams (3-10 users)
**Features**:
- Up to 10 users
- Unlimited tasks
- 5 GB storage per user
- Email support (48h response)
- Real-time collaboration
- Task comments

- Basic analytics

- Advanced analytics & reporting
- Custom fields
- Automation rules
- API access
- Integrations (Slack, GitHub, etc.)
- Audit logs
- SSO (Single Sign-On)

- Everything in Professional
