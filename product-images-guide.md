- Use 1200x630px for LinkedIn optimal sizing
- Ensure high contrast and readable text
- Add subtle branding (logo/colors) consistently
- Keep UI elements large enough to be visible in social media feeds
- Use PNG format for crisp screenshots
- Consider adding subtle drop shadows or borders for better visibility on white backgrounds
